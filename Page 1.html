A Brief Summary Of Electronic Sports

ESports is a word for online sports and multiplayer tournaments. Although it is not considered a sport, eSports get hundreds of thousands of viewers with million dollar prize pools. The usage of streaming services like Twitch and MLG has increased the popularity and amount of viewers that spectate the tournaments. First person shooter games have less viewership and lower prize pools because of the rise of MOBAs, real time strategy games, and fighting games.

ESports Viewing Platforms

Twitch.tv is a streaming website that broadcasts all genres of eSports, excluding CallofDuty. It also allows pro players or regular players to stream regular gameplay.
MajorLeagueGaming.com is a website that streams CallofDuty eSports and pro player gameplay.
YouTube is a platform that some eSport organizations post tournament info, gameplay, and hype videos to get more viewers on the streaming websites.
